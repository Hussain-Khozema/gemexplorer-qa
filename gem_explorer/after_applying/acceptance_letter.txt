Acceptance Letter
After successful application, students would be notified by their host universities regarding their Acceptance Letters.  OGEM will notify students to collect the hard copies of their Acceptance Letters accordingly (if available). 

Type of Acceptance Letter
Soft copy: By Email from host university/OGEM

Hard copy: Collection at OGEM's office 

Application Outcome (including Acceptance Letter) from Host University:
Semester 1: March to July

Semester 2: August to December
